About the project

So, this is my first big project inspired by Jesse Metzger (https://github.com/jmetzg11). He gave me two weeks to build good looking web app as a challenge to prove that I am not a lazy bum. Later on, at 2 am I was thinking about what gist of my application would be already for a while, when an idea of creating app where 4 useful things that user can do would be stacked at one place came to my mind.
